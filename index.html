Speaking practice

Chrome/Edge use the built-in recognizer; Arc, Brave and Safari automatically switch to an on-device AI model (one-time download, then cached — your voice never leaves your machine). Your recording is playable so you can compare with the native audio.

Pinyin typing

See the characters, type the pinyin with tone numbers — e.g. 你好 → ni3 hao3 (spaces optional). Neutral tone is 5 (or 0), type v for ü. Right sounds + right tones = 100%; right sounds, wrong or missing tones = 50%.
